Pricing/TOS

Terms of Service

Payment

Commissions may be payed 50% of the final cost initially if requested. After the sketch is completed, you are free to give feedback. At this time, 50% will be due. The final piece will be on hold until the payment is received. (This does not apply to sketch commissions.)

Refunds will not be applicable unless stated otherwise by the artist.

Commissions may take up either 2 days up to a week. This may vary due to school and other disturbances. The artist will let you know if there is a known delay or if you are on a waitlist.

Guidelines

The artist has the right to watermark the final piece for upload onto social media sites, unless stated otherwise by commissioner.

If the commissioner asks for offensive and/or foul content, their commission will be cancelled and be blacklisted. This includes but is not limited to: racism, pedophilia, sexism, zoophilia, and hate towards the LGBTQIA+ community.

You must NOT claim the artist's work as your own as well as edit it (unless given permission). If this rule is broken, you will be added to my blacklist.

Payments will be sent to given PayPal in USD.

If you commission a full-body commission, a ref sheet will be needed. This may be worked around if sufficient references are given.
